Keyboard/focus and screen-reader notes

  • Every interactive primitive has a visible focus ring sourced from semantic.border.focus.
  • Modals trap focus, restore focus on close, and expose title/description.
  • Loading and success are polite live regions; errors are assertive live regions.
  • VoiceOver/TalkBack labels are required for icon-only controls and status badges.
  • Dynamic Type/font scaling must preserve text wrapping and tap target size.

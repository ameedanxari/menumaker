🎯 PHASE 2 FEATURES (6 User Stories × Detailed Acceptance Criteria)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  1. WhatsApp Order Notifications & Status Updates
     Priority: P1 | Effort: 8 days
     • Seller connects WhatsApp Business Account
     • Real-time order notifications on WhatsApp
     • Status updates sent to customers via WhatsApp
     • Retry logic for failed messages (3× exponential backoff)

  2. AI-Assisted Menu Import (OCR & Text Parse)
     Priority: P1 | Effort: 10 days
     • Upload menu image → OCR extracts text
     • AI structures to {name, description, price, category}
     • Preview & manual correction before import
     • Allergen auto-suggestion
     • Time to import 10 dishes: < 2 min (vs. 10 min manual)

  3. Templated Legal Copy & Privacy Policy Generator
     Priority: P1 | Effort: 4 days
     • Jurisdiction-specific templates (India, US, UK)
     • Customizable refund, T&C, privacy policy
     • Published on public menu footer
     • Versioning & audit trail

  4. Integrated Payment Processing (Stripe & PayPal)
     Priority: P1 | Effort: 12 days
     • Seller connects Stripe account via OAuth
     • Customer pays via card, Apple Pay, Google Pay
     • Order auto-marked paid after Stripe webhook
     • Seller receives payout (2.9% + $0.30 fee)
     • Refund flow supported
     • Monthly settlement report

  5. Tiered Subscriptions & Free Trial
     Priority: P1 | Effort: 10 days
     • 3 tiers: Free (5 dishes, manual pay) | Pro (unlimited, Stripe) | Business (all Pro + OCR)
     • All new sellers default to 30-day free trial of Pro
     • Monthly recurring billing (Stripe Billing API)
     • Feature limits enforced per tier
     • Trial expiry → auto-downgrade to free

  6. Customer Re-order & Saved Carts
     Priority: P2 | Effort: 6 days
     • Return customers see "Previous Orders" tab
     • One-click re-order (same dishes + qty)
     • "Modify" option to customize before ordering
     • Cart persistence (browser local storage)
     • Re-order time reduced: ~2 min → ~20 sec

�� PHASE 3 FEATURES (9 User Stories × Detailed Acceptance Criteria)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  1. Multiple Payment Processors (Razorpay, PhonePe, Paytm)
     Priority: P1 | Effort: 15 days
     • Connect 1+ processors simultaneously
     • Customer chooses processor at checkout
     • Processor-specific fees displayed (1.75–2.36%)
     • Automatic failover if processor unavailable
     • Monthly settlement reconciliation

  2. Automated Tiered Payouts & Payout Scheduling
     Priority: P1 | Effort: 10 days
     • Daily/weekly/monthly payout frequency
     • Minimum threshold (e.g., "hold if < Rs. 500")
     • Automatic bank reconciliation
     • Failed payout retry logic
     • Subscription fee deduction shown per payout

  3. Multi-Language Support & RTL Layout
     Priority: P1 | Effort: 14 days
     • UI translations: English, Hindi, Tamil, Arabic
     • RTL layout for Arabic/Hebrew
     • Locale-specific date/time/currency formatting
     • Form validation messages translated
     • Screen reader support (WCAG compliant)

  4. Advanced Reporting & Tax Compliance
     Priority: P2 | Effort: 12 days
     • Tax invoice generation (GST-compliant)
     • GST reports (monthly outward supplies)
     • Profit analysis (revenue - expenses dashboard)
     • HSN/SAC codes auto-applied
     • GSTIN field (for registered sellers)
     • Compliance data export per date range

  5. Review & Complaint Workflow
     Priority: P2 | Effort: 10 days
     • Post-order review email (valid 7 days)
     • Rating (1–5 stars) + review text + photos
     • Seller can respond publicly
     • Ratings < 3 flagged as complaints
     • Review moderation (24-hour window)
     • Seller profile shows avg rating + reviews

  6. Marketplace & Seller Discovery
     Priority: P2 | Effort: 16 days
     • Homepage: "Browse All Sellers"
     • Filters: cuisine, rating, distance/location
     • Seller cards: name, rating, top dishes, distance
     • Search: text search for sellers/dishes
     • Seller opt-in for discoverability (privacy control)
     • Featured sellers (editorial or top-rated)
     • Marketplace analytics (impressions, conversions)

  7. POS System Integration (Square, Dine, Zoho)
     Priority: P2 | Effort: 18 days
     • Seller OAuth to Square/Dine/Zoho
     • Orders auto-synced to POS in real-time
     • Sync failure handling (retry, local backup)
     • Reverse sync: inventory levels back to MenuMaker
     • Audit: seller can view sync history

  8. Delivery Partner Integration (Swiggy, Zomato)
     Priority: P3 | Effort: 12 days
     • Seller registers with delivery partner (outside MenuMaker)
     • Orders routed to delivery partner system
     • Delivery tracking shown in MenuMaker
     • Separate delivery rating
     • Delivery cost: seller absorbs or passes to customer

  9. Promotions, Coupons & Discounts
     Priority: P3 | Effort: 8 days
     • Seller creates coupons (fixed or %)
     • Coupon rules: date range, usage limits, min order value
     • QR code generation (shareable on social)
     • Automatic promotions (e.g., "free delivery > Rs. 500")
     • Coupon redemption analytics
